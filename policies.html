Financial Policies

All revenue is distributed around the company to cover costs
such as property expenses, raw material and manufacturing expenses,
and salaries for employees. Most of the remaining profit is reinvested
in the growth of BioSurf.

Delivery Policy

All unsuccessful deliveries will be refunded and the customer
will be able to purchase another product at half price. Any unsettling
interactions with delivery drivers can be reported and will be
investigated to determine the accuracy of the report.

Return Policy

At BioSurf we offer a 60 day warrantee on all boards.We also accept returns
on all boards and wetsuits contigent upon them being in a reasonable
condition. Purchases of BioWax are non-refundable.
