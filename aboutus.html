Origins of Biosurf

Founders and co-owners, brothers Michael and Devon Smith grew up on the south shore of Long Island New York. There they spent most of their time in the ocean, surfing big waves and having fun. However, shredding through surfboards as often as they did, the Smith brothers found that they were spending thousands of dollars on boards! After doing a little research they found out that the high cost of surfboards was due to the rare and expensive wood that is used for most boards. This means that not only were boards becoming more and more expensive, but there also wasn't a sustainable source of materials to create them. Michael and Devon decided that it was time to create a surfboard that was both affordable and sustainable, so in 2016 they founded BioSurf, the first completely sustainable surfboard company. Upon opening there was only one location in Smithtown NY, and stock was limited to roughly 30 boards of varying sizes. By 2022 the company has grown exponentially with new stores opening in Long Beach NY, Port Jefferson NY, and Montauk NY. New stock has also hit the shelves such as the BioWetsuit which is made completely out of entirely recycled material and the brand new BioWax: an organic, eco friendly surfboard wax.

Why Choose Biosurf?

-Sustainable Products

Every one of our products here at Bio Surf are 100% sustainable in
their production and manufacturing. Our boards are made with a recycled
EPS foam exterior and a hemp fiberglass core ensuring the best of both
worlds for weight and maneuverability, while also giving you the peace
of mind of not harming the earth.

-Affordable Products

Our boards are sold at rates hundreds of dollars lower than boards
sold by different comanies. With prices ranging from 80-120$ it's hard
for companies who are still using wood to compete. Surfboards of comparable
quality to ours could go for 300$ or 400$, if made out of more expensive
alternatives.

-Friendly, Local Staff

Our staff is comprised of some of the friendliest islanders you could
come to know. With many employees being from around the area of their store,
they're happy to help you with tips on local surfing spots and swells.
